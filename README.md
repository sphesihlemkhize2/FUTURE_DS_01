Business Sales Perfomance Analysis Report

Project Overview
This project focuses on transforming raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence. Using a combination of Python for data cleaning and Power BI for interactive visualization, I analyzed the retail data to identify growth drivers, seasonal trends and high-value geographic markets.

Tools used
Python(pandas), Power BI desktop, DAX

Key Insights from my dashboard
The November peak: Revenue shows a very steady climb in August, peaking at 1.1M in November. This could suggest a strong Black Friday or holiday season impact.
Market dominance: The United Kingdom accounts for the majority of total revenue, which signalls a highly centralized market.
Product Performance: After the analysis of the best 10 selling products, the Paper Craft Little Bird is a top-tier revenue generator, which significantly outperforms middle-range inventory items.

Strategic Recommendations
Market Diversification: With the UK dominating sales at 82% compared to the whole market, the business is at risk if the UK economy slows. I would then recommend targeted marketing in Netherlands and EIRE (Ireland) where there is already a baseline of interest.

Inventory Optimization: Since the top 10 products drive a significant portion of revenue, the warehouse should prioritize on ensuring that there is always stock availability for these specific items to prevent lost sales during peak months, therefore having more in advance to cater for these seasons.

Seasonal Staffing: Based on the InvoiceDate trends, the business should increase customer supportby employing more employees and logistics capacity starting in September to prepare for the season which is Q4.
